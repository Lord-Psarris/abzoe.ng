EMG/NCS SOLUTIONS
NIGERIA

There has been a dearth of academic research and clinical diagnoses involving electromyography [EMG] and nerve conduction studies [NCS] in Nigeria despite a rising increase in demand. This has primarily been due to a chronic shortage of trained personnel as well as limited availability and access to the necessary equipment within the country.

To this end, ABZoe Solutions plans to provide nationwide services using mobile EMG/NCS machines from December 2021 - February 2022 to increase engagement and exposure to EMG and NCS in both academic and clinical institutions.


Agreements will be reached with interested entities from each institution/city regarding the dates of deployment. This will reduce the overall cost of deploying the solution.


EMG/NCS operations will be handled by internationally certified personnel

ELIGIBLE ENTITIES

    This solution is for:
  • Universities, hospitals, colleges, institutes, laboratories, etc
  • Practitioners, lecturers, clinicians, researchers and students of Medicine, Physiotherapy, Physiology, Exercise Physiology, Biology, Neuroscience, Human Kinetics, etc

ELIGIBLE SERVICES

Diagnosis of muscle and nerve disorders ; neurorehabilitation and neuromuscular rehabilitation; exercise and training measurements; and neuromuscular research.
